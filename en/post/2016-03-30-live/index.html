March 30th 2016, live: "My Funny Valentine"

May 4, 2016 · 1 minute read · Comments

This recording was made during a beautiful night dedicated to Chet Baker, entitled Chet & Delp by the renowned producer Oscar Daniel Chilkowski, who invited me to take part in what happened to be the debut of this group.

Chet & Delp:

  • Martin Delp vocals
  • Nicola B. Bernardelli flügelhorn
  • Miguel Marengo piano
  • Juan Manuel Bayón double bass
  • Quintino Cinalli drums

If you can set the player for HD, you get a better sound quality, YouTube streams better audio together with better video.

My Funny Valentine by Richard Rodgers and Lorenz Hart

www.youtube.com/watch?v=pXR62ls4uT0


Recording: Zoom H2n with the built-in mics
Processing: Ableton Live Suite
