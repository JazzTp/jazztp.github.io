February 3rd 2016, live: "Adiós Nonino"

Feb 26, 2016 · 1 minute read · Comments

Néstor Astarita was finally with us on February 3rd, here we are again in Notorious jazz club, Buenos Aires, Argentina.

This version of Adiós Nonino alternates delirant and lyric moments. Pablo was fighting with an audio feedback problem, he was often turning to the equipment in an attempt to solve it, but I find that the Larsen effect adds a nice Jimi Hendrix touch to this take :D

The Néstor Astarita quartet:

  • Nicola B. Bernardelli trumpet and flügelhorn
  • Alejandro Kalinoski piano
  • Pablo Tozzi double bass
  • Néstor Astarita drums

If you can set the player for HD, you get a better sound quality, YouTube streams better audio together with better video.
